About Me

I believe that the creative process is a never-ending path that if we let it flourish will takes us to places we never expected we could arrive. I love finding shapes, colours and ideas to fit peoples styles and bring my own perspective, to deliver the pieces that will make them feel and look amazing when they wear my clothes. I know the impact feelings play in someone's life and I want to bring the right ones to the people wearing my creations, creations for every genre, every body type and every age.
